September 22 Starts a New Class of The Iron Yard Houston Students

By Brian Dorton , Campus Director at The Iron Yard Houston.

We are so very excited to begin the journey of changing lives with our incoming class of students. Monday, September 22 is the first day in an arduous 12 week adventure that will end with our Rails and Javascript warriors ready to take on whatever challenge awaits them. I want to welcome all of the new students and congratulate them on taking the first step towards realizing their dreams!

We had a blast meeting new students at our open house on Wednesday while enjoying some delicious Chipotle burritos. I really enjoyed hearing everyone's back story and what brought them to The Iron Yard. On behalf of the entire team Iron Yard team here in Houston, I can say I truly can't wait to see what these rock stars are going to be able to accomplish.
